description=[h3]Features[/h3]
description=[quote]
description=[list]
description=[*]Auto-opens [b]doors[/b] and [b]multi-tile gates[/b] while [b]running[/b] and/or [b]sprinting[/b].
description=[*]Respects vanilla rules: [b]barricades, keys/padlocks, obstructions[/b] (won’t bypass locks).
description=[*]Optional [b]auto-close[/b] (only closes doors/gates opened by this mod). [b]WARN:[/b] Not recommended for Multiplayer due to potential desync.
description=[*][b]Custom Breach Animation:[/b] Your character now physically kicks/bashes the door open while moving. (MP Synced: other players see you kick the door).
description=[*][i]Note: I am not a professional animator. If the animation looks odd or causes issues, you can disable it in Sandbox Options.[/i]
description=[*][b]Stats & XP:[/b] Breaching now costs a small amount of [b]Endurance[/b] (scales with Fitness) and grants [b]Fitness XP[/b].
description=[*][b]Breach shove (optional) – Build 42 only:[/b] when the mod opens a door/gate, it can [b]push[/b] nearby zombies and sometimes [b]knock them down[/b].
description=[/list]
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[h3]Sandbox Options[/h3]
description=[quote]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]AutoCloseDoor[/b] – enable/disable auto-close for mod-opened doors/gates. [i](default: Off)[/i]
description=[*][b]WhileRunning[/b] – enable/disable auto-open while running. [i](default: On)[/i]
description=[*][b]WhileSprinting[/b] – enable/disable auto-open while sprinting. [i](default: On)[/i]
description=
description=[h3]Animation & Stats[/h3]
description=[*][b]EnableAnimation[/b] – Enable/Disable the custom kick animation. [i](default: On)[/i]
description=[*][b]BreachCost[/b] – Base endurance lost per breach. [i](default: 0.01)[/i]
description=[*][b]FitnessReducesCost[/b] – If enabled, higher Fitness reduces endurance cost. [i](default: On)[/i]
description=[*][b]FitnessXPGain[/b] – XP gained in Fitness per breach. [i](default: 2.5)[/i]
description=
description=[h3]Shove Physics (Build 42)[/h3]
description=[*][b]ShoveNearbyZombies[/b] – enable/disable breach shove when opening. [i](default: On)[/i]
description=[*][b]ShoveRange[/b] – shove radius (tiles). [i](default: 1.8 | range: 0.0–10.0)[/i]
description=[*][b]ShoveAngle[/b] – shove cone angle (degrees). [i](default: 120.0)[/i]
description=[*][b]KnockdownChance[/b] – chance (%) to knock down. [i](default: 35)[/i]
description=[*][b]EnablePropagation[/b] – enable/disable shove wave propagation. [i](default: On)[/i]
description=[*][b]PropagationDepth[/b] – how many tiles forward the wave can continue. [i](default: 2)[/i]
description=[*][b]PropagationStrength[/b] – chance (%) for the wave to affect next zombie. [i](default: 60)[/i]
description=[/list]
description=[/quote]
